About the Conference

The International Conference on Complex Dynamics: Modeling and Simulation brings together leading researchers, scientists, and practitioners from around the world to discuss the latest advancements in complex systems theory and its applications.

This three-day event will feature keynote lectures, technical sessions, and networking opportunities, providing a platform for knowledge exchange and collaboration in this rapidly evolving field.

The conference will be held at EST Essaouira, Morocco, from September 25th to 26th, offering participants not only a stimulating scientific program but also the chance to experience the rich cultural heritage of this beautiful coastal city.

Venue

EST Essaouira

The École Supérieure de Technologie d'Essaouira (EST Essaouira) is a prestigious institution of higher education located in the beautiful coastal city of Essaouira, Morocco.

Known for its excellent facilities and commitment to scientific research, EST Essaouira provides the perfect setting for this international conference.

Essaouira, with its rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning Atlantic coastline, offers conference participants a unique opportunity to combine scientific exchange with cultural exploration.
